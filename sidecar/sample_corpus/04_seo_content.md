Search traffic compounds. A well-researched article can drive leads for years after publication with minimal maintenance.

## Keyword research fundamentals

Start with questions your audience actually asks. Tools like search console data, "People Also Ask" boxes, and competitor gap analysis reveal intent better than raw volume numbers.

## Content depth vs frequency

Publishing three deep, authoritative pieces per month outperforms daily thin posts. Search engines reward topical authority — clusters of related articles that cover a subject comprehensively.

## Technical foundations

Fast page loads, clean heading hierarchy, descriptive URLs, and proper meta descriptions are table stakes. Content quality cannot compensate for a site that takes five seconds to render.

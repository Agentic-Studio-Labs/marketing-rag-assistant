Personas anchor tone and proof. When the assistant retrieves chunks, persona metadata helps prioritize stories that match the buyer you are writing for.

## Awareness-stage content

At awareness, lead with problem language and category education. Avoid heavy product feature lists; focus on outcomes and tradeoffs your reader recognizes.

## Consideration-stage content

At consideration, mix comparison framing with concrete proof: timelines, team prerequisites, and integration patterns reduce perceived risk.

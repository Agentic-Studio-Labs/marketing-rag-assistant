Well-structured chunks improve retrieval. Use headings to keep each segment thematically coherent so embeddings capture a single idea.

## Why heading-aware chunking matters

Fixed-size windows often split concepts across boundaries. Readers—and vector search—lose the thread when half a headline sits in one chunk and the proof points sit in the next.

## Metadata you can filter on

Tag chunks with content type, persona, and funnel stage so you can narrow search to “case studies for enterprise buyers in decision stage” without re-embedding the entire library.
